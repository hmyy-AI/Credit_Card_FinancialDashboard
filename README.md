# Credit_Card_FinancialDashboard
Power bi Dashboard
Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of credit card performance, focusing on revenue, customer behavior, transaction activity, and risk metrics.
The goal is to help stakeholders monitor business performance and identify key drivers of growth.
Key Insights
Week-over-Week (WoW)
Revenue increased by 28.8%, indicating strong short-term growth momentum
Total transaction amount and transaction count increased, showing higher customer spending activity
Customer count increased, suggesting expansion in the active user base
Year-to-Date (YTD) Overview
Total revenue reached 57M, with 8M generated from interest
Total transaction amount recorded at 46M
Male customers contribute higher revenue (**31M**) compared to female customers (**26M**)
Blue and Silver credit cards account for **93%** of total transactions, indicating high concentration in these segments
Top contributing states include TX, NY, and CA, making up 68% of total revenue
Overall activation rate stands at 57.5%, reflecting moderate customer engagement
Delinquency rate is 6.06%, indicating manageable credit risk
Recommendations
Improve customer activation rate to drive higher revenue contribution
Reduce dependency on Blue and Silver cards by promoting other card types
Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions (TX, NY, CA)
Develop targeted strategies to increase engagement among underperforming customer segments
Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modeling
SQL Server
Notes
Data is analyzed on both Week-over-Week and Year-to-Date basis
Metrics are calculated using aggregated transactional data
Dashboard is designed for business monitoring and decision-making support
